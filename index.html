What is Programming?

Programming is when a person provides (a computer or other machine) with coded instructions for the automatic performance of a task. What this means is that programming is essentialy the action of people when they communicate with computers to give it instructions to do a task.

There are a multitude of ways people can communicate effectivly with a computer. There are many "languages" for many differnt tasks and functions.
